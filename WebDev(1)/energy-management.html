Energy Management

Solar Power

Los Santos harnesses the power of the sun with a massive network of solar panels. This clean, renewable energy source supplies a significant portion of the city's electricity, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

Wind Energy

The city's wind farms provide an additional source of renewable energy. These towering turbines harness the wind's natural power, helping to meet the energy needs of the city in an eco-friendly manner.

Smart Grids

Los Santos operates a highly efficient smart grid system that ensures seamless distribution of electricity. The smart grid optimizes energy distribution, minimizes power loss, and balances supply with real-time demand.

Energy Storage

Excess energy generated from solar and wind sources is stored in state-of-the-art battery systems. These energy storage solutions ensure that power is available even during times of low renewable generation, guaranteeing uninterrupted supply.
